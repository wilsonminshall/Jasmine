            <p><br>Three sisters walk amongst mortals,</br> <br>Their mission one of retribution or justice.</br> <br>One is unceasing, driven, and relentless.</br> <br> Another for vengeance and wrath.</br>  <br>The last a creature of bitter resentment,</br> <br>Never to forget the deeds of evil men.</br> <br>Call down their curse,</br> <br>That revenant of suffering,</br> <br>Relentless, vengeful, and unforgiving.</br> <br>Wielding pestilence and famine,</br> <br>And should you fail to atone</br> <br>They'll drag you to hell with glee.</br> <br>Sorrow so easily warps itself,</br> <br>molded by the harshness of man.</br> <br>In its place is rage, hateful and cruel.</br> <br>These sorrowful sisters emerge from flame,</br> <br>Begins of fury and punishment...</br> <br>So call upon them, and bestow their curse.</br></p>
