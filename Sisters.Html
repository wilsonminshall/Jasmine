Ekphrasis 2023 - Poem by   Tierney Privett


Three sisters walk amongst mortals,

Their mission one of retribution or justice.

One is unceasing, driven, and relentless.

Another for vengeance and wrath.

The last a creature of bitter resentment,

Never to forget the deeds of evil men.

Call down their curse,

That revenant of suffering,

Relentless, vengeful, and unforgiving.

Wielding pestilence and famine,

And should you fail to atone

They'll drag you to hell with glee.

Sorrow so easily warps itself,

molded by the harshness of man.

In its place is rage, hateful and cruel.

These sorrowful sisters emerge from flame,

Begins of fury and punishment...

So call upon them, and bestow their curse.
